About me

Hi Kihara Tenkei here, you can called me “kira” , I often makin arts about my ocs . Warning, i DO draw cringe art for fun sometimes. Currently, I’m also expanding my creative toolkit by learning to code, unlocking new possibilities for interactive design and digital art. Some of my ocs will be used in future for my work it could be comic ,games. The world i built is not for all people and it can contain some serious problems. What i draw is only for fun or dark joke that’s don’t meant to harm any people Feel free to explore my arts and my characters , don’t steal my artwork or repost it if you don’t credit it.
