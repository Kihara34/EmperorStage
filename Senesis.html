Lore

Through years of learning and giving abundance to others, along with her brother Abandomaris and her sisterFarahsark, the dull land gradually became stronger and more majestic than ever. She was the one who contributed to repelling “The Afternoon War I” and created “Liquid Senalloy” , which helped cure the "Blackest Bloom" disease.
But then the greed and ambition of mortals became more and more excessive, they made her more and more tired and sick, weighing down on her shoulders, their high expectations and vain wishes were truly despicable
The last straw was when the people she helped were the ones who voted for her sister Farahsark's execution, from then on her faith in humanity was shattered into a hundred pieces, she reversed theGolden Trust into Golden Faith, deceived them with blind faith and blinded them with shining gold, single-handedly invaded and oppressed other lands so that they would understand hunger, poverty and suffering, turning greedy subjects into stupid puppets hoping to appease and avenge her sister's death.

Notes

  • Although she was an extremely intelligent and knowledgeable person, she had already seen that something was wrong with Farahsark's death, but her pain and disappointment in humanity had caused her to lose her composure.

  • She sometimes thought about talking to her brother again, but she still found it hard to accept his coldness towards Farahsark's death.

  • Most of her accessories are made of Senalloy, only the stone on her chest was given to her by Farahsark and she still wears it to this day.

  • She doesn't have a specific weapon, she mainly attacks by creating and manipulating Senalloy, but she is nonetheless a knowledgeable and intelligent being from which she can use many types of weapons but still mainly a staff taller than her with a pointed tip that branches out in 8 directions to help pierce and amplify the attacks that create Senalloy.

Extra

  • She has many golden marks spread all over her body from neck to toe.

  • Her left eye was injured but not completely blind, she covered it to hide what she considered a defect, normally all her injuries could be healed quickly but because she was attacked by “Blade of The Black Moon Fall", it caused the recovery process to be delayed for a long time.

  • She is an arrogant and self-confident person, her every word is full of contempt and malice, she knows her great position, knows her limits and grace but she does not become subjective because of that, she is the representative of the mind, all her moves and strategies are always calculated in a sophisticated and cunning way, no one can succeed in understanding or tricked her except Farahsark

Born in the early ages around 1150 years ago, Senesis is a gift from “The Great Land” , “The Representative of Faith” in charge of the power of Mind, the abundance and fullness of gold, she carries within her the innocence and generosity towards the mortals under her command.
