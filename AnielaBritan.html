Lore

Aniela Britan was raised by her mother to be a walking weapon — an angel shaped for battle. That was until she met Sacrina Eunoia, the Angel of the Library. Their first encounters were marked by hostility, but over time, Aniela's sharp edges softened under Sacrina's influence, revealing the person she truly was.
The two fought side by side during the war — Aniela known as the Angel of Benevolence, Sacrina as the Angel of Harmony. When the war ended, Sacrina invited Aniela to join her in the library.
